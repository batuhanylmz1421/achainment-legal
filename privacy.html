Privacy Policy for aChainment

Last Updated: March 2026

Batuhan Yılmaz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the aChainment mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We built the aChainment app as a Freemium app. We collect and use information to provide and improve the App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1.1 Local Storage

By default, all your goals, chains, and progress data are stored locally on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not transmit your local tracking data to our own databases.

1.2 Google Drive Integration (Cloud Sync)

If you choose to use the "Cloud Sync" feature (available to PRO users), the App requests access to your Google Drive via your Google Account.
Requested Scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata

  • What we access: We strictly access a hidden, app-specific folder (Application Data folder) within your Google Drive to store a JSON backup of your chain progress.
  • We CANNOT see, read, or modify your personal Google Drive files (e.g., your photos, documents, or emails).
  • This data is transferred directly between your device and Google's servers. We do not route this data through any intermediary servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2.1 Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

To support the free version of the App, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and other tracking technologies (like cookies or advertising IDs) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your location and consent.

  • For more information on how Google uses data for advertising, please visit: Google's Privacy & Terms

2.2 Google Play Billing

If you choose to upgrade to aChainment PRO, the payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play (or our verified proxy, RevenueCat). We never see, process, or store your credit card information or direct billing details.

3. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer contact email listed on the Google Play Store page.
